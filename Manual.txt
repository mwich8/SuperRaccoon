You can either play this game on PC by using the "Space"-key or play it on an Android phone(for which it was developed) by tapping on the screen.
Jumping: Spacebar/Tap once
If you collected enough energy you can buy ugrades. Only one upgrade at a time might be activated.
Dark Coon:
	Stamp on the ground: Tap if you're in the air
Light Coon:
	Double jump: Tap if you're in the air
